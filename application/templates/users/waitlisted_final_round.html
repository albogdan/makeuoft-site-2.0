MakeUofT 2020 Application Decision

The MakeUofT team has reviewed your application, and due to both the high caliber and high volume of applications, we have decided not to grant you a guaranteed spot at MakeUofT. We offer no guarantee that you will be allowed into the event, regardless of how early you arrive. Please read our participant package for all the info regarding the event if you plan on waiting to see if any spots become available.
